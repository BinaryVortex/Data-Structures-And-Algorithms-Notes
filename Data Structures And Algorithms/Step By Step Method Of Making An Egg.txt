Step By Step Method Of Making An Egg.

1)Heat the pan over medium heat for 1-2 minutes.

2)Add 1 teaspoon of butter or oil and let it melt.

3)Crack the egg into a small bowl, then slide it gently into the pan.

4)Cook the egg undisturbed for 2-3 minutes (cover for faster cooking, if desired).

5)Season with salt and pepper, and check the whites are set but the yolk is runny.

6)Gently lift the egg with a spatula and transfer it to a plate.

7)Serve and enjoy!
